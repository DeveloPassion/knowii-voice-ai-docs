# Knowii Voice AI

**Knowii Voice AI** is a privacy-first voice-to-text transcription application that runs entirely on your computer. Your voice never leaves your device.

## Why Knowii Voice AI?

- **Privacy First**: All processing happens locally. No cloud required. Your data stays on your device.
- **Simple to Use**: Press and hold a shortcut key to record, release to transcribe and paste. That's it!
- **Fast & Accurate**: Powered by state-of-the-art AI models with GPU acceleration support.
- **Works Everywhere**: Transcribe into any application - emails, documents, chat, notes, browsers, IDEs.
- **Completely Offline**: No internet connection needed. No tracking. No telemetry.

## Quick Start

1. **Download** Knowii Voice AI from Gumroad or from the [Knowii Community](https://www.knowii.net) website, depending on where you bought it from
2. **Install** and launch the application
3. **Download** your first AI transcription model (guided by the setup wizard)
4. **Press and hold** `Ctrl+Shift+G` to record your voice
5. **Release** to transcribe and automatically paste the text

That's it! You're ready to go.

## Key Features

### Voice-to-Text Made Simple

Press and hold a keyboard shortcut to record your voice. Release to automatically transcribe and paste at your cursor position. Works in any application.

### Privacy Preserving

Everything happens on your computer. No internet required. Your voice and transcriptions never leave your device.

### Smart & Fast

GPU acceleration, automatic microphone detection, and high-quality transcription powered by cutting-edge AI models.

### History & Management

View all past transcriptions, star favorites, search history, and even play back audio recordings.
