# Support

Need help with Knowii Voice AI? I'm here to assist you!

## Before Reaching Out

Before contacting support, please check these resources:

- **[FAQ](./faq)**: Common questions and answers
- **[User Guide](./user-guide/installation)**: Detailed documentation and guides
- **[Getting Started Tutorial](./tutorials/getting-started)**: Step-by-step walkthrough
- **[Roadmap](./roadmap)**: Upcoming features and planned improvements

## How to Get Help

### Email Support

For direct support, email me at:

**[support@knowii.net](mailto:support@knowii.net)**

I respond personally to all support requests. No ticket systems, no chatbots - just direct human support from me.

### Knowii Community

Join the Knowii Community to:

- Ask questions and get help from other users
- Share tips and best practices
- Request features and provide feedback
- Stay updated on new releases

**[Visit the Knowii Community](https://www.knowii.net)**

Look for the space dedicated to Knowii Voice AI to connect with other users and get community support.

### Report Issues on GitHub

Found a bug or have a feature request? Report it on GitHub:

**[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/DeveloPassion/knowii-voice-ai-docs/issues)**

When reporting an issue, please include:

- Your operating system and version
- Knowii Voice AI version
- Steps to reproduce the problem
- Expected vs. actual behavior
- Any error messages or logs

### Documentation Feedback

If you find errors or have suggestions for improving the documentation:

**[Create a Discussion on GitHub](https://github.com/DeveloPassion/knowii-voice-ai-docs/discussions)**

## Response Times

- **Email Support**: I aim to respond within 24-48 hours on business days
- **Community**: Response times vary based on community activity
- **GitHub Issues**: I review and triage issues regularly

## What to Include in Your Support Request

To help me assist you more quickly, please provide:

1. **Your environment**:
    - Operating system (Windows 10/11, Linux, macOS)
    - Knowii Voice AI version
    - AI model being used

2. **The problem**:
    - What you were trying to do
    - What happened instead
    - Steps to reproduce the issue

3. **Screenshots or logs** (if applicable):
    - Error messages
    - Settings screenshots
    - Log files (if relevant)
